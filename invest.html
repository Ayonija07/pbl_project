Understanding Investment Risk

  • 📌 Risk vs Return: Higher risk investments offer higher potential returns but greater loss possibility.
  • 📌 Low Risk: Bonds, Fixed Deposits (FDs) - Safe but lower returns (~4-6%).
  • 📌 Medium Risk: Mutual Funds, Mixed Portfolio - Balanced approach (~8-12%).
  • 📌 High Risk: Stocks, Crypto - High potential returns but volatile (~15%+).
